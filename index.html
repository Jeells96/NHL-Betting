How it works

Expected 1st period goals = average of (home team's 1st period scoring + away team's 1st period defense) and (away team's 1st period scoring + home team's 1st period defense).

Expected full game total uses the same symmetric formula on season scoring rates. Edge is the difference between expected and your line.

Confidence tiers: HIGH ≥ 0.7 edge · MED ≥ 0.4 edge · LOW ≥ 0.2 edge · PASS under 0.2.

Data sources

NHL schedule and standings from the official NHL API, refreshed on demand.

1st period scoring estimated at 29% of each team's total scoring rate (NHL historical average).
