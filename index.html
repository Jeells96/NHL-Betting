How it works

Expected 1st period goals = average of (Home team P1 GF/GP + Away team P1 GA/GP) and (Away team P1 GF/GP + Home team P1 GA/GP).

Expected total goals uses the same symmetric formula on full-game scoring rates. Edge is the difference between expected and the line.

Confidence tiers: HIGH ≥ 0.7 · MED ≥ 0.4 · LOW ≥ 0.2 · PASS under 0.2.
